Arizona Income Tax Overview

Arizona has an effective state income tax rate of approximately 2.5%.

Example: $75,000 Salary in Arizona

  • Federal tax: ~$8,200
  • Arizona state tax: ~$1,875
  • FICA (SS + Medicare): ~$5,738
  • Annual take-home: ~$59,188
  • Bi-weekly paycheck: ~$2,276

Living and Working in Arizona

Arizona residents should consider the overall tax burden including federal income tax, state income tax, property taxes, and sales taxes when evaluating their take-home pay.

Tips for Arizona Residents

  • Maximize pre-tax deductions — 401(k) and HSA contributions reduce your taxable income.
  • Check your withholding — Use the IRS W-4 calculator to avoid overpaying or underpaying.
  • Consider local taxes — Some cities and counties add additional income taxes.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the state income tax rate in Arizona?

Arizona has an effective state income tax rate of approximately 2.5%. This is on top of the federal income tax which ranges from 10% to 37%.

How much will I take home in Arizona?

On a $75,000 salary in Arizona, your approximate bi-weekly take-home pay is $2,276 after all taxes and deductions. Use the calculator above for your exact situation.

Are there local taxes in Arizona?

Some cities and counties impose additional income taxes. Check with your local tax authority for details specific to your area within Arizona.
